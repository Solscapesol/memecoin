Game Rules

  1. The game is played in 60-second rounds.
  2. Each round is a 1v1 match between two players.
  3. The primary objective is to loot as much bitcoin as possible from the treasure chest within the 60-second time limit.
  4. The player who collects the most bitcoin from the chest wins the round.
  5. If one player dies during the round, that player loses automatically, regardless of the amount of bitcoin looted.
  6. Players are not allowed to respawn once dead.
  7. The round ends when either the 60 seconds expire or one player dies.
  8. In case of a tie (if both players collect the same amount of bitcoin), the player with higher HP wins.
